Who we are

Worry opened its doors in the hoods of Lowell, Massachusetts. A group of rigorous, hardworking, and persevering college students opened a unique brand the world had never seen before. Lighting up the way to new trends and styles, Worry was built on the footwork of some of the hardest working beings in existence. Not just hard laborers, but minds-of-a-million. Funded on debt ridden pockets, today Worry has become not only a brand, but a lifestyle. Uniting gamers and weeaboos alike, Worry has proven that anything is possible with effort. Through the hands of pioneers, engineers, designers, and artists, we bring you Worry.
